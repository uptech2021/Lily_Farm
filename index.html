Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about our lilies and services.

🌿 What types of lilies do you offer?

We offer a wide variety of exotic lilies and aquatic plants — from tiger lilies and oriental hybrids to lotus flowers and day-blooming water lilies. Browse our full collection →

🚚 What are your delivery charges?

Delivery rates are based on your location: Central Trinidad — $60, North Trinidad — $85, South Trinidad — $60. Free delivery on orders over $500.

💧 Are your lilies pesticide-free or organic?

Yes, absolutely! All our flowers are grown using organic, eco-friendly farming practices — no harmful chemicals, ever.

🌸 Can I place an order for pickup?

Yes! You can select Pickup at checkout and collect your order from our farm in Central Trinidad. We'll confirm your pickup time via email or phone.
