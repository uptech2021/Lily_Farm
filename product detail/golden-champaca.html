Description:
The Golden Champaca is a fragrant tropical tree known for its stunning golden-yellow blossoms and rich, sweet aroma. Perfect for home gardens, it thrives in full sun and adds elegance to any landscape.

Fertilizer Information:
Apply a balanced 10-10-10 fertilizer every 6 weeks during the growing season. Water regularly and ensure soil is well-drained for healthy growth and abundant flowering.
