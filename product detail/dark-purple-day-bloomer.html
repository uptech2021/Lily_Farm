Description:
The Dark Purple Day Bloomer is a striking tropical water lily with deep violet petals that shine brilliantly under sunlight. Its rich color and broad green leaves make it a stunning focal point in any pond or water garden.

Fertilizer Information:
For best results, use aquatic plant fertilizer tablets such as 10-14NPK once every 3-4 weeks during the growing season. Push the tablet gently into the soil near the plant roots. Ensure the Lily receives full sunlight for maximum blooming.
