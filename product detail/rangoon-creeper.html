Price: TTD $100.00

Description:
The Rangoon Creeper is a vibrant tropical vine that changes from white to pink to deep red as they mature. It thrives in full sunlight and brings a lush, colorful beauty to fences and garden walls.

Fertilizer Information:
Feed every 4 to 6 weeks with a balanced fertilizer (10-10-10 NPK or 14-14-14 NPK). Use organic compost fertilizer to promote lush foliage and flowering. Ensure good drainage and moderate watering for healthy growth.
