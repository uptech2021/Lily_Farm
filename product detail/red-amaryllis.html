Description:
The Red Amaryllis is a striking tropical flower known for its large, trumpet-shaped, deep red blooms. It adds vibrant color to gardens and makes a bold statement as a potted plant indoors or outdoors.

Fertilizer Information:
Use a balanced, water-soluble fertilizer (10-10-10 or 5-10-10) every 2–3 weeks during active growth. Stop fertilizing after the bloom period to allow the bulb to rest. Always apply fertilizer after watering to avoid root burn.
