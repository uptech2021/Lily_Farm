White Purple Day Bloomer

Price: TTD $150.00

Description:
The White Purple Day Bloomer is an elegant tropical water lily featuring delicate white petals accented with soft purple hues near the center. It opens in full sunlight, adding a colorful charm to ponds and water gardens.

Fertilizer Information:
Feed with slow-release aquatic fertilizer tablets every 4 to 6 weeks during the warm months. Push the tablets into the soil near the plant’s roots. Avoid applying fertilizer directly into the water.
