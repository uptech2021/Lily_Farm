Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about our lilies and services.

🌿 How do I care for my lilies after purchase?

Keep lilies in indirect sunlight, change water every two days, and trim stems slightly for longer freshness.

🚚 Do you offer delivery?

Yes! Island-wide delivery within 2–3 business days. Free delivery on orders over $100.

💧 Are your lilies pesticide-free?

Absolutely! Our flowers are grown organically using eco-friendly farming practices — no harmful chemicals, ever.

🌸 Can I visit the farm?

Yes! Book an appointment via our Contact page. Tours available Tue–Sat, 9am–4pm.
